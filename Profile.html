Group Medical Insurance – Parent(s)

Eligibility

As an Accenture India employee, you can purchase this benefit at your own cost.

You can cover:
  • Both parents, if chosen (only father and mother) will be covered in the same Sum Insured without any age capping. (if you identify yourself as Lesbian, Gay, Bi-Sexual or Transgender). Grandparents or any elderly relative such as a maternal or paternal aunt/ uncle will not be eligible for coverage under this policy.
Coverage is effective or ends at:
  • New Hire: Coverage begins on the date of joining if elected within 30 days. The Premium will be pro-rated from the date of joining for the remaining policy period based on the installments chosen.
  • Promotion: No increase/change to the coverage will be allowed due to a mid policy year promotion
  • Exit: The coverage will continue until the end of the policy period (31 March). There will be no refund of premium.
  • Leave of Absence: Cover continues through the Leave of Absence (LoA) period.
  • Life Events:
    • Add new dependents (marriage/ birth, etc.): Coverage begins on the date of life event if elected within 30 days. The Premium will be pro-rated from the date of life event for the remaining policy period based on the instalments chosen.
    • Cancel Dependents (parents’ divorce/ end of relationship with your partner): Coverage will continue till the end of the policy period. No premium will be refunded for the remaining policy period. However, a separate new policy can be taken for the newly acquired dependent, if any.
    • Cancel Dependents (dependents reach maximum covered): Coverage ends automatically at the end of the policy period.
    • Cancel Dependents (death of a dependent): Coverage ends as of the date of the life event. No premium will be refunded
Note: It is your responsibility to update the life event in the enrollment section. You need to visit the enrollment section and update the details of the dependent change within 30 days of the life events.
